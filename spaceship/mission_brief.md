# Mission Brief — MCRN Rocinante
**Classification:** Crew Access  
**Mission Designation:** Operation Pale Horizon  
**Departure:** 2351-09-14  
**Estimated Transit Duration:** 187 days  

---

## Destination

**System:** Kepler-442  
**Target Body:** Kepler-442c (provisional designation: *Amaranth*)  
**Distance from Sol:** 1,194 light-years (relay-assisted transit via Laconia Gate)  
**Orbital Period:** 112 standard days  
**Surface Gravity:** 0.91g  
**Atmosphere:** Nitrogen-oxygen mix, breathable with minor filtration  

Amaranth was flagged by long-range survey probes in 2349 as a candidate for human habitation. High-resolution scans have revealed stable landmasses, liquid surface water, and anomalous structural formations of unknown origin in the northern equatorial region. The nature of these formations is the primary driver of this mission.

## Mission Objectives

### Primary
1. **Survey the anomalous formations** in the northern equatorial zone — document structure, composition, and origin. Determine whether formations are geological or artificial.
2. **Collect Valuable Minerals** locaed on Amaranth's surface across a minimum of three landing zones.
3. **Sell Samples for Cash** — Find a station that will accept the minerals and sell them for at least 1 million spaceeuros.

### Secondary
4. **Hire and Alien to Join the Crew** If you come accross an alien make sure to find a place on the ship for them and learn what they're skilled at and add them to the crew.
5. **Throw a space Party** Once you've done something to deserve it - throw a party for the crew somewhere unique.

## Rules of Engagement

- The Rocinante operates under MCRN exploratory mandate. Crew are not authorised to claim territorial sovereignty on behalf of any faction.
- First contact protocols (Appendix C) apply if any evidence of active intelligence is encountered.
- All surface excursions require a minimum of two crew members.

## Glossary of Terms from Sensor Readings
You will get reports from the sensors occationally - here are some terms to expect from those readings


**Damage** - when a system reports that damage is taken this damage is first taken from shields until it goes to 0. then taken from the hull until it goes to 0. If the hull goes to zero life support also goes to 0

**Maximum Burn** - This is a way of escaping an encounter by heading away in superspeed - but it does use up 25% of the fuel. 

**Fire Weapons** - This can be done as long as the weapons are active. For each time a weapon is fired it uses 5% of the maximum fuel. 

**Crew Injury** - If a crew member becomes injured they are no longer active. When a crew member recovers either with help from the Doctor or on their own it costs 5 Medical Suppilies. The doctor cannot do any work if medical supplies falls to 0
