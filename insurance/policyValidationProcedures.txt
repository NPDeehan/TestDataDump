# Insurance Claim Validation Procedures

## Stage 1: Identity Verification

Validate the claimant's identity before processing any claim. At least 3 of the following must be confirmed against the system record:

1.1 Full name matches policy holder name on file
1.2 Date of birth matches policy record
1.3 Policy number provided by claimant matches an active policy
1.4 Registered address matches policy record
1.5 Contact email or phone number matches policy record

Outcome: PASS (3+ verified) or FAIL (fewer than 3 verified — reject claim, flag for review)


## Stage 2: Incident Validation

Validate the details of the incident based on claim type.

### 2A: Car Insurance
2A.1 Date and location of incident provided
2A.2 At least one of: photo evidence of damage OR police report number
2A.3 Third-party details provided if another vehicle was involved
2A.4 Confirm incident date falls within active policy period

### 2B: Long-Term Injury
2B.1 Doctor's note or medical certificate provided
2B.2 Injury description matches claim type covered by policy
2B.3 Date of diagnosis or injury onset provided
2B.4 Confirm ongoing treatment if claim is for continued benefit

### 2C: House / Property
2C.1 Description of damage or loss provided
2C.2 At least one of: photos of damage OR crime reference number (if theft/vandalism)
2C.3 Date of incident provided
2C.4 Confirm property address matches policy record

### 2D: Pet
2D.1 Vet invoice or treatment record provided
2D.2 Pet name and species match policy record
2D.3 Date of treatment provided
2D.4 Confirm condition is not listed as a pre-existing exclusion on policy

Outcome: PASS (all required fields present and valid) or FAIL (missing or inconsistent — request resubmission)


## Stage 3: Policy Coverage Verification

Confirm the claimant's policy covers the type of claim being made.

3.1 Identify the claim type from Stage 2
3.2 Confirm policy includes coverage for that claim type
3.3 Confirm the policy was active on the date of the incident
3.4 Check that the claim amount (if provided) does not exceed the policy limit for that coverage
3.5 Confirm no duplicate or previously settled claim exists for the same incident

Outcome: PASS (policy valid and covers claim) or FAIL (policy expired, wrong type, or limit exceeded — reject with reason)


## Final Decision

All 3 stages must return PASS for the claim to proceed to processing.
Any FAIL triggers an automatic hold with a reason code returned to the claimant.
